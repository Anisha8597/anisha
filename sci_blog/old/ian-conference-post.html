Reflections from the Neuroscience Conference (IAN) 2024

November 12-14, 2024 6 min read

The Indian Academy of Neuroscience (IAN) Conference represents one of the premier neuroscience gatherings in India. For anyone interested in pursuing a PhD in neuroscience or related fields, this conference offers an invaluable opportunity to immerse yourself in cutting-edge research and connect with leading minds in the field.

Why Attend IAN?

For aspiring researchers and PhD candidates, IAN provides a platform not just to learn, but to showcase your work. Presenting a poster at this conference can significantly enhance your CV and provide visibility among established researchers. The experience of explaining your research and fielding questions from experts offers tremendous growth as a scientist.

Notable Speakers and Research Highlights

The 2024 conference featured several prominent researchers whose talks illuminated various corners of neuroscience:

  • Dr. Pankaj Seth delivered an insightful presentation on viral mechanisms in neurological disorders, detailing how certain viruses interact with neural pathways
  • Dr. Vivek Tiwari from IISER shared his groundbreaking work on neural circuit development
  • Dr. Saurav Banerjee from National Brain Research Centre (NBRC) presented fascinating findings on neuroplasticity mechanisms
  • Dr. Poonam Thakur gave an engaging talk on neurodegenerative disease pathways and potential therapeutic targets

Beyond these selected talks, the conference covered an impressive breadth of neuroscience topics across the three days. Sessions ranged from schizophrenia research and anxiety disorders to neuroimaging advances and computational neuroscience approaches.

Conference Experience

The organization committee deserves commendation for their meticulous planning. The schedule ran smoothly with timely presentations across three parallel conference rooms, allowing attendees to select sessions most relevant to their interests. The quality of food provided was exceptional—a detail that shouldn't be overlooked when spending long days absorbing complex information!

Looking Ahead

The next IAN conference is scheduled for 2025 in Trivandrum, which promises to be another enriching gathering. For anyone considering a career in neuroscience research in India, marking this event on your calendar would be a wise decision.

Personal reflection: Attending IAN 2024 reinforced my passion for neuroscience research and provided clarity on emerging directions in the field. The networking opportunities alone made the conference worthwhile, creating connections that may shape future collaborations.
